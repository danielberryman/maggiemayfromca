Maggie made her West End début with the original cast of Sister Act the Musical at the London Palladium. She understudied and had the privilege to play the role of Sister Mary Robert alongside Whoopi Goldberg when she joined the cast, this time as Mother Superior. She then flew to New York to perform in the Sister Act Broadway Press Launch with Whoopi and composer, Alan Menken.

Returning to the Palladium, Maggie performed in Andrew Lloyd Webber’s revival of The Wizard of Oz, starring Michael Crawford (alternating her time playing a Munchkin and flying around Oz as Glinda the Good Witch). She was one of ten actors chosen to workshop the show with Lord Webber himself before it premiered on the West End.

Maggie trained at The Royal Academy of Music in London after earning her Bachelor’s Degree at the University of California, Davis. She grew up in Sacramento, California where began performing at a young age with local organizations including River City Theatre Company, The Sacramento Children’s Chorus and California Musical Theatre’s renowned Music Circus.
